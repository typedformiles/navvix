A Raspberry Pi 5 running moOde Audio as a hi-fi music streamer, paired with Blissify for algorithmic playlist generation based on actual sonic analysis of the music library. No cloud, no tracking, no subscription.

Includes a custom remote display built on a Pi Zero 2 W showing album art and track info in real time.

[Read about the display project](/blog/moode-audio-remote-display/) and [the Blissify setup](/blog/blissify-algorithmic-playlists-for-offline-music/).
