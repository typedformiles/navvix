A VideoWeek interview where I discussed why the perceived data challenges in connected TV are often bigger in people's heads than they are in practice. Clean room technology and deterministic measurement were already solving a lot of the problems that buyers were citing as reasons not to invest in CTV.

This was during my time at LiveRamp, working on the clean room side of the business and seeing first-hand how the data infrastructure for TV was evolving faster than the market's perception of it.
