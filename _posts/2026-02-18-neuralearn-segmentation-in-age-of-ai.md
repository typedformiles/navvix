An educational video I put together for [Neuralift](https://neuralift.ai) covering how segmentation is evolving from a descriptive exercise to one of automated discovery. Deep learning, LLMs, and GPU-powered computation are all making their way out of the science lab and into our daily marketing stacks.

This isn't about having an AI send emails for you whilst you sleep. It's a practical dive into the bedrock of marketing - segmentation - and how these technologies are changing what's possible. Big thanks to Jonathan Mendez and Mike Maloney at Neuralift for their own approach to knowledge sharing since I joined the company.

Yes, the name NeuraLearn is terrible but it was the best I could think of.
